About 2100 Global News

Our Mission

2100 Global News is an independent, multi-planetary news agency dedicated to accurate, neutral, and factual reporting across Earth, orbital habitats, and off-world colonies.

Our mission is to document global developments with clarity and responsibility in an era defined by rapid technological, political, and environmental transformation.

Editorial Principles

  • Commitment to factual accuracy
  • Political and technological neutrality
  • Clear separation between reporting and analysis
  • Transparency in sources and data interpretation

Global Presence

Our correspondents operate across multiple regions and jurisdictions, ensuring comprehensive coverage of planetary affairs.

Earth Headquarters: Geneva, Earth Federation
Lunar Bureau: Mare Tranquillitatis, Moon
Mars Bureau: Olympus City, Mars

Coverage Scope

2100 Global News covers a wide range of topics, including global governance, technological innovation, planetary expansion, artificial intelligence policy, and long-term human development.
